About Us

Our Mission is to protect and improve the lives of the furry animals by sheltering, caring, hosting adoption services, rescuing, and educating the community.

Our Vision is to end overpopulation and create a community where all furry animals are placed in a permanent and loving home that treats them with love and respect.

Furry Friends Animal Shelter is a nonprofit, No Kill shelter that cares for dogs and cats of all ages. We are currently only accepting dogs and cats, however, we are planning on expanding our home for smaller furry animals in the future.
