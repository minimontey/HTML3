Furry Friends Animal Shelter is a No Kill shelter that cares for dogs and cats of all ages. Our mission is to find the perfect owner and forever homes for our furry friends. We are currently only accepting dogs and cats, however, we are planning on expanding our home for smaller furry animals in the future.

Thinking about adopting a pet? Click on "About" to find your perfect match! 💗

Adoption fees for Dogs & Cats:
Puppy Under 1 Year - $135
Dog 1 Year and Over - $50
Kitten Under 1 Year - $125
Cat 1 Year and Over - $45

Adoption fee includes spay/neuter surgery, first set of vaccinations, microchip, de-wormer, flea and heartworm prevention
